description

you’ve probably heard of scrapbooking, and junk journaling is pretty similar. both are super fun and relaxing to do!

materials needed

  • journal
  • glue stick
  • hot glue gun
  • tape
  • anything you wanna put in the book

directions

junk journaling and scrapbooking are a little bit different so here are descriptions of both. scrapbooking is mostly photos with things around them to decorate the pages. junk journaling is more random things. the page in the photo is a spread I did with random stickers I had laying around my bedroom. I have another spread that is a bunch of stuff I collected when I went to new york (tickets, menus, etc). for both options, there is no specific way to do it and the goal is to make memories and make it look pretty!
