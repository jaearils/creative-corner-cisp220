welcome to the creative corner!

hello! welcome to the creative corner! this website is for anyone that crafts or is simply bored and needs something fun to do. most of the projects on here use things that you probably have around your home so you don't even have to go out and buy anything! our goal is to make crafting fun and easy for everyone! :)

I've always liked doing craft projects in my free time, especially when I'm bored or stressed. it is sometimes really hard to find ideas that are simple and don't require a bunch of supplies, so I wanted to create a space where everything is easy and relatively simple. on this site, you will find different types of crafts and hobbies that are beginner friendly and not overwhelming so anyone can try it! i want this site to be a place where people can find simple, fun ideas without feeling overwhelmed. crafting should be relaxing, not stressful, and i hope this site helps with that.

a little bit about me...I'm julia acker and I am the owner of the creative corner! I am 17 years old and live in michigan. I am an early college student at davenport university and getting my associates degree in computer information systems. I love doing crafts and little hobbies in my free time so I figured I would share some of my favorite things to do!
