description

phone charms are a little craft you can do to decorate your phone! they can be made pretty fast and you can change them out whenever you want to match your outfits or mood that day.

materials needed

  • beads
  • some sort of string (i use fishing line because it’s clear but you can use anything)

directions

there are a few different ways you can make the charm. the first is just a simple hanging charm and the second is a circle chain. to make a simple one (like the one in the picture), you just put beads on the string and make a loop to attach it to your phone. to make a circle chain, you string enough beads to go both up and down your phone. then you tie it in a circle (so it kind of makes a big bracelet) and then attach it to your phone. to attach to your phone, I just make a loop and put it on the small part of my case by the ringer switch.
