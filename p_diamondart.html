description

diamond art and bedazzling have become really popular over the last few years, and for a good reason. it can be time consuming and the possibilities are endless!

materials needed

  • gems
  • glue
  • diamond art pen tool or tweezers

directions

you can either buy a kit to make certain things (like the suncatcher I made in the picture), or you can bedazzle anything! just put glue on a surface and then put your diamonds. it can be hard to make the diamonds all line up but once something is fully bedazzled, it is super pretty! some things that I’ve seen people bedazzle are book covers, pop cans, makeup caps, and even legos!
